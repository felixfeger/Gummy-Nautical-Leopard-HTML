Your Journey Starts Here

Follow these simple steps for a smooth and stress-free commute with City MetroLink.

1

Purchase Your Ticket

Use station kiosks, or online portal (Coming Soon) to buy your fare before boarding. Always keep your ticket ready for inspection.

2

Check Real-time Status

Monitor our live tracker or station displays for the latest arrival times and platform assignments.

3

Board the Train

Wait behind the yellow line. Allow passengers to exit before boarding. Find a seat or hold onto the rails if standing.

4

Smooth Transfers

Need to switch lines? Our major hubs provide clear signage and integrated schedules for effortless transitions.

Top Travel Tips

  • Arrive at least 5 minutes before scheduled departure.
  • Sign up for SMS alerts for instant service updates.
  • Bikes are welcome on all trains in designated areas.
